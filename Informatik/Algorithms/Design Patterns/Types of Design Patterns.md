### Types
+ Architectural Patterns
	+ fundamental structural patterns for whole architecture
	+ e.g. Layers, Pipes and Filters, Broker, Model-View-Controller, ...
+ Design Patterns
	+ solve more isolated problems
	+ e.g. Composite, Adapter, Proxy, Factory, ...
+ Idioms
	+ fine-grained patterns for problems in specific environments
	+ Concurrency
		+ Locking
	+ Messaging Family
